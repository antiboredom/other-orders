Other Orders

Other Orders is a tool for sorting text. It offers 27 different sorting mechanism, following several methodologies. New sorts may be added over time, and suggestions/requests are welcome.

You may try Other Orders online with your twitter feed, or by downloading a python script to run on your own texts.
